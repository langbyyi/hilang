<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">1. 什么是 RCE？</span></h3>
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">RCE （Remote Command/Code Execute） 即远程代码执行和远程命令执行的统称，指攻击者向后台服务器注入操作系统命令或代码，从而控制后台系统，分为远程命令执行和远程代码执行，属于高危漏洞，常被统称为 RCE。</span></p>

<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">2. 远程命令执行漏洞原理是什么？</span></h3>
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">核心原理是</span><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">应用程序未对用户输入进行严格过滤</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">，将用户可控输入直接拼接进系统命令执行函数（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>system</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>exec</code></span><span class="md-plain">），导致攻击者注入的恶意命令被系统执行。</span> <span class="md-plain">例如：应用程序通过</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>system(&quot;ping &quot; + $_GET[&#039;ip&#039;])</code></span><span class="md-plain">执行 ping 命令，若用户输入</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>127.0.0.1;ls</code></span><span class="md-plain">，则拼接后命令变为</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>ping 127.0.0.1;ls</code></span><span class="md-plain">，其中</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>;ls</code></span><span class="md-plain">会被作为新命令执行，最终执行</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>ls</code></span><span class="md-plain">查看文件。</span></p>

<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">3. 远程代码执行漏洞原理是什么？</span></h3>
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">原理是</span><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">应用程序将用户可控输入直接作为代码执行</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">（未过滤或过滤不严），导致攻击者注入的恶意代码被应用程序解析执行。</span> <span class="md-plain">例如：PHP 中若存在</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>eval(&quot;echo &quot; . $_GET[&#039;input&#039;]);</code></span><span class="md-plain">，用户输入</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>1;phpinfo();</code></span><span class="md-plain">时，代码会被拼接为</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>eval(&quot;echo 1;phpinfo();&quot;);</code></span><span class="md-plain">，进而执行</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>phpinfo()</code></span><span class="md-plain">函数，泄露服务器信息。</span></p>

<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">4. 代码执行的函数有哪些？</span></h3>
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">代码执行函数指直接将输入作为代码解析执行的函数，分语言举例：</span></p>

<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">PHP：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>eval()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>assert()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>preg_replace()</code></span><span class="md-plain">（带</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>/e</code></span><span class="md-plain">修饰符）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>array_map ()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>create_function()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>call_user_func ()</code></span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p md-focus"><span class="md-plain">Python：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>exec()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>eval()</code></span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">Java：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>ScriptEngine.eval()</code></span><span class="md-plain">（如 Rhino 引擎）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>Runtime.exec()</code></span><span class="md-plain">（间接执行字节码）</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">JavaScript：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>eval()</code></span></p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">5. 命令执行的函数有哪些？</span></h3>
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">命令执行函数指直接调用操作系统命令的函数，分语言 / 场景举例：</span></p>

<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">PHP：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>system()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>exec()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>shell_exec()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>passthru()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>popen()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>proc_open()</code></span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">Python：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>os.system()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>os.popen()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>subprocess.call()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>subprocess.Popen()</code></span></p>

 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">系统层面：Linux 的</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>system()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、Windows 的</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>CreateProcess()</code></span></p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">6. php 能远程执行的函数有什么？</span></h3>
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">PHP 中可用于远程执行（代码 / 命令）的函数主要包括：</span></p>

<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">代码执行</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>eval()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>assert()</code></span><span class="md-plain">（5.4 前）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>create_function()</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>preg_replace(&#039;/pattern/e&#039;, ...)</code></span><span class="md-plain">（PHP 7.0 前）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>call_user_func()</code></span><span class="md-plain">（动态调用执行函数）。</span></p>

 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">间接执行</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>include()</code></span><span class="md-plain">/</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>require()</code></span><span class="md-plain">（远程包含文件时执行代码，需</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>allow_url_include=On</code></span><span class="md-plain">）。</span></p>

<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">7. RCE 漏洞有什么危害、利用方式及防御措施？</span></h3>
<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">危害</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span> <span class="md-plain">攻击者可完全控制目标系统（如读取 / 篡改文件、窃取数据）、横向渗透内网、植入恶意程序（如勒索软件）、瘫痪服务等，是最高危漏洞之一。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">利用方式</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span> <span class="md-plain">直接执行命令（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>ls</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>whoami</code></span><span class="md-plain">）获取信息；写入 webshell 持久化控制；通过命令提权（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>sudo</code></span><span class="md-plain">、内核漏洞）获取更高权限；下载恶意文件（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>wget</code></span><span class="md-plain">）扩大影响。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">防御措施</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span></p>

<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">输入严格过滤：用白名单限制允许的字符 / 命令，禁止特殊符号（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>;</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>|</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>&amp;</code></span><span class="md-plain">）。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">避免直接拼接执行：使用安全 API（如参数化调用，避免</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>system(&quot;cmd &quot; + input)</code></span><span class="md-plain">）。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">最小权限运行：应用程序以低权限用户（如 www-data）运行，限制文件 / 系统访问权限。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">禁用危险函数：通过</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>php.ini</code></span><span class="md-plain">的</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>disable_functions</code></span><span class="md-plain">禁用</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>eval</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>system</code></span><span class="md-plain">等。</span></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">8. 一个站点存在 RCE 漏洞，如何 getshell？如何利用 webshell 进行连接？</span></h3>
<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">Getshell 方法</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span> <span class="md-plain">利用 RCE 执行命令写入 webshell 文件。例如：</span></p>

<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">若为 Linux，执行</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>echo &#039;&lt;?php @eval($_POST[&quot;cmd&quot;]);?&gt;&#039; &gt; /var/www/html/shell.php</code></span><span class="md-plain">（写入 PHP 一句话木马）。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">若为 Windows，执行</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>echo ^&lt;?php @eval($_POST[&quot;cmd&quot;]);?^&gt; &gt; C:\www\shell.php</code></span><span class="md-plain">（注意转义特殊符号）。</span></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">连接 webshell</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span> <span class="md-plain">使用工具（如蚁剑、菜刀、冰蝎）输入 webshell 的 URL（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>http://target.com/shell.php</code></span><span class="md-plain">）和密码（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>cmd</code></span><span class="md-plain">），工具通过 POST 请求发送命令（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>$_POST[&quot;cmd&quot;]=phpinfo();</code></span><span class="md-plain">）与 webshell 交互，实现文件管理、命令执行等操作。</span></p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">9. 想要查看文件时发现 cat 被过滤了，怎么办？</span></h3>
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">核心思路是用</span><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">替代命令</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">或</span><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">绕过过滤规则</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">，常见方法：</span></p>

<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">替代命令：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>more file</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>less file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（分页查看）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>head file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（查看头部）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>tail file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（查看尾部）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>nl file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（带行号）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>od -c file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（八进制显示）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>vi file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（编辑模式查看，:q 退出）。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">绕过过滤：</span></p>

<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">大小写混淆：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>Cat file</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>CAT file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（若系统不区分大小写）。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">变量绕过：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>a=c;b=a;c=t; $a$b$c 文件名</code></span><span class="md-plain">。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">编码进制绕过：对命令进行 base64 等编码处理后执行，如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>echo &#039;Y2F0wqAK&#039; | base64 -d 文件名</code></span><span class="md-plain">。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">字符拼接：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>c&#039;&#039;at file</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>c$1at file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（shell 会忽略空字符或变量）。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">转义字符：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>c\at file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（反斜杠转义）。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-plain">通配符：</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>ca? file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>?</code></span><span class="md-plain">匹配单个字符）、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>c* file</code></span><span class="md-plain">（</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>*</code></span><span class="md-plain">匹配多个字符）。</span></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="md-end-block md-heading"><span class="md-plain">10. 如何绕过 php.ini 中 disable_function 的限制？有哪些方法？其中成功率最高的是哪个？为什么？</span></h3>
<ul class="ul-list" data-mark="-">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">常见方法</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：</span></p>

<ol class="ol-list" start="">
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">LD_PRELOAD 劫持</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">（Linux）：上传自定义共享库（.so 文件），通过</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>putenv(&quot;LD_PRELOAD=/tmp/evil.so&quot;)</code></span><span class="md-plain">预加载，劫持</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>system</code></span><span class="md-plain">等函数实现命令执行。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">PHP 扩展漏洞</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：利用</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>imap</code></span><span class="md-plain">、</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>gd</code></span><span class="md-plain">等扩展的漏洞（如</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>imap_open</code></span><span class="md-plain">的命令注入）绕过限制。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">FFI 扩展</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：若启用</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>FFI</code></span><span class="md-plain">（Foreign Function Interface），可直接调用系统函数执行命令。</span></p>
</li>
 	<li class="md-list-item">
<p class="md-end-block md-p"><span class="md-pair-s "><strong><span class="md-plain">Windows COM 组件</span></strong></span><span class="md-plain">：在 Windows 系统中，通过</span><span class="md-pair-s" spellcheck="false"><code>COM(&quot;WScript.Shell&quot;)</code></span><span class="md-plain">调用系统命令。</span></p>
</li>
</ol>
